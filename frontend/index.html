About Us

Our Mission

Portfolios mission is to encourage young creators to display their work. A place for people to feel encouraged and inspired, and meet other likeminded individuals.

The Developers

We are three hardworking developers striving to create beautiful and functional websites for our projects. Teamwork is an important attribute and no matter what hurdles come our way we get over them.


The Project

'Portfolio' was created for Yoobees Web and UX Formative 3.2. This Formative helped us to work and problem solve as a team, and helped us learn a lot about Mongo, Gulp, GitHub, and Postman.
